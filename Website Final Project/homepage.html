Who are we?

The ECG Code Club was founded in 2020 at the Early College at Guilford. Our goal is to promote STEM, specically Computer Science and Engineering, in our local community. We host competitions at school, as well as camp events like this one. We believe that everyone should have access to resources to learn about programming.
